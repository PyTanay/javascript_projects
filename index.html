Located in the plant premises at GNFC, Bharuch Technical Services department boasts of skilled chemical engineers and varied work portfolios. Along with smooth functioning and debottlenecking of plants, Tech Services also functions to monitor performance of critical equipments in the complex. Technical data submission to Gov, management reports, weapons report of GNFC are also handled by Tech Services Dept.

Simulation Tools

In addition to manual calculations TS uses following simulation tools to solve complex problems related to process engineering.

Aspen Plus

For steady state process simulations.

HTRI

For exchanger simulations!

Chemcad

For quick process simulations!
